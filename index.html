BIO

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of California, Los Angeles advised by Kai-Wei Chang. I am supported by the Eugene V. Cota-Robles Fellowship. My research interests are in NLP fairness and robustness, specifically for low resource languages.

Previously, I received my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Physics from Emory University in 2022.
